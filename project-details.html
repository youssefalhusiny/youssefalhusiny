PROJECT DETAILS

This artistic photography project explores the concept of urban solitude through minimalist composition and monochromatic aesthetics.

Through careful attention to light and shadow, each frame captures the quiet poetry of urban spaces, transforming ordinary scenes into contemplative visual narratives that invite viewers to pause and reflect.

CREDITS

  • Photography: youssef alhusiny
  • Art Direction: youssef alhusiny
  • Location: Urban Landscapes

SPECIFICATIONS

  • Project Budget: $15,000 - $20,000
  • Timeline: 3 Months
  • Deliverables: 25 Final Images
  • Equipment: Phase One XF, Prime Lenses
