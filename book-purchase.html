About This Book

Many famed music producers are known for a particular sound that has its day. Rick Rubin is known for something else: creating a space where artists of all different genres and traditions can home in on who they really are and what they really offer. He has made a practice of helping people transcend their self-imposed expectations in order to reconnect with a state of innocence from which the surprising becomes inevitable.

What You'll Learn

  • How to tap into your creative potential and overcome creative blocks
  • The importance of being present and aware in the creative process
  • Techniques for developing your unique artistic voice
  • Understanding the relationship between artist and audience
  • Methods for maintaining creative momentum throughout your career

Perfect For

This book is essential reading for anyone interested in the creative process—whether you're a musician, writer, visual artist, designer, or simply someone who wants to live a more creative life. Rick Rubin's insights transcend any particular medium and speak to the universal experience of creating.
